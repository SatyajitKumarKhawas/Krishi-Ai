Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer?

Click on the "Register" button and fill out the form with your basic information. You'll need a valid mobile number for verification.

Is the AI service really free?

Yes, Kerala Krishi AI is completely free for all registered farmers. This is a government initiative to support agriculture.

Can I ask questions in Malayalam?

Absolutely! Our AI system supports Malayalam language queries. You can type, speak, or upload images with Malayalam descriptions.

What if the AI can't answer my question?

Complex queries are automatically escalated to our expert agricultural officers who will provide personalized guidance.
